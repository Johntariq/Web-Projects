Our Legacy of Tailoring

For over 31 years, Andaz-e-Khas Tailor's has been a cornerstone of bespoke ladies' fashion in Lahore. Founded by my father, a master craftsman with a passion for traditional Pakistani attire, our business was built on a simple promise: to deliver unparalleled quality and a perfect fit for every client.

We believe that a dress is more than just fabric; it's a form of expression. From the intricate embroidery on a bridal lehenga to the graceful fall of a casual shalwar kameez, every detail is crafted with meticulous care. Our small, dedicated team of artisans shares this vision, combining age-old techniques with contemporary styles to create garments that are both timeless and modern.

We are proud to continue this tradition, helping women across Pakistan feel confident and beautiful in their custom-made attire. Thank you for being a part of our journey.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I place a custom order?

You can place an order through our "Custom Order" form. Please provide as much detail as possible, including dress type, fabric, and any design references. Alternatively, you can use the "Order by Contact" form, and we will call you to discuss the details.

2. What is the average turnaround time for an order?

Typically, a casual dress takes 7-10 days, while formal or bridal wear can take 3-6 weeks, depending on the complexity of the design and embroidery. We will provide a more accurate timeline once your order is confirmed.

3. What are the payment methods?

We require a 50% advance payment to confirm your order. The remaining 50% is due upon completion before delivery. We accept bank transfer, EasyPaisa, and JazzCash. Payment details will be shared once your order is finalized.

4. Can I provide my own fabric?

Absolutely! We are happy to work with fabric provided by you. Please ensure the fabric is of good quality and sufficient for the desired design. You can drop it off at our Lahore workshop.

5. What if my dress requires alterations?

We strive for a perfect fit based on the measurements you provide. However, if minor alterations are needed, we offer one complimentary alteration session within 7 days of you receiving the dress. Major changes to the design will be charged accordingly.

6. Where is your workshop located?

Our workshop is located at 123-C, Model Town, Lahore. While we primarily operate online, clients can schedule an appointment for in-person consultations or fabric drop-offs.
